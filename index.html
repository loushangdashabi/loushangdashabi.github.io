About

I am a MPH student at Vanke School of Public Health, Tsinghua University, advised by Dr. Zhihui Li.

Prior to this, I obtained a BM in Public Health from Zhejiang University, with a minor in Public Administration through the selective Undergraduate Program at Chu Kochen Honors College.

My current research focuses on investigating the effect of preprimary education legislation.
